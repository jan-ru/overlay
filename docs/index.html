üîß How to Use:

  1. Navigate to your HvA calendar page
  2. Select your course (Operations or GRC) from the dropdown
  3. Click overlay buttons to show/hide visual overlays:
    • Blok - Shows the entire blok period (weeks 36-44)
    • Sprint 1 - Weeks 36-37
    • Sprint 2 - Weeks 38-40
    • Sprint 3 - Weeks 41-44
  4. Today's emphasis - If today falls within the selected period, it will be highlighted with a darker overlay
  5. Toggle overlays - Click the same button again to remove the overlay
